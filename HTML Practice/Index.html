How to Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

  1. Two pieces of bread, your favorite peanut butter and jam or jelly
  2. Spread Peanut Butter on one side
  3. Spread the jelly either on top of peanut butter or spread onto the other piece of bread
  4. Place the two pieces together and enjoy!
